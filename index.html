A landing page for the fastest land animal

The cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus) is a large cat with a tawny to creamy white or pale buff fur that is marked with evenly spaced, solid black spots.

Cheetah Facts

Most wild cheetahs live in sub-Saharan Africa

Cheetahs are social animals and are commonly found in groups

They hunt during the day to avoid other predators

They can grow up to 1.4m long and weigh up to 54kg
